**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Comprehensive dataset of key health indicators for Venezuela (2020-2025). Includes mental health metrics (depression, anxiety, suicide rates), healthcare workforce decline, infectious disease burden (malaria, dengue, HIV, measles), non-communicable d*.
